# Deleting Table

Follow the steps to delete a table:

1. **Navigate to Your Database**: Log in to your Rocket account and select the desired database from the database selector on the top left.

2. **Access Tables**: Click on the "Tables" link in the left navigation to proceed.

3. **Select Table**: Choose the desired table from the tables list.

4. **Initiate Deletion**: Click on the delete button located at the left side of the selected table's tile.

5. **Confirm Deletion**: Upon clicking the delete button, a confirmation window will appear. To proceed with the deletion, click on the "Delete" button within this window.

By following these steps, you can effectively delete a table from your Rocket database.
