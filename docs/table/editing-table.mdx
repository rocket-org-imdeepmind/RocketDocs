# Editing Tables

Follow these steps to edit a table:

1. **Navigate to Your Database**: Log in to your Rocket account and select the desired database from the database selector on the top left.

2. **Access Tables**: Click on the "Tables" link in the left navigation to proceed.

3. **Select Table**: Choose the desired table from the tables list. Click on the right-side button to open the table.

4. **Click on Edit Button**: Click on the edit button located at the top right corner to begin editing the table.

5. **Provide Necessary Details**:
   - **Table Name**: Update the table name.
   - **Description**: Update the table description.
6. **Edit Table**: Upon completing the requisite details, finalize the process by clicking the "Submit" button.
