# Sign-up and Registration

1. **Visit Registration Page**: To get started, go to [https://app.rocketapi.net/auth/register](https://app.rocketapi.net/auth/register) for the account registration page. You need to provide your name, email, and password to create a new account. Once you submit the form, you'll receive an OTP (One-Time Password) in the provided email to complete the account creation process.

2. **Pricing**: By default, Rocket automatically selects the Bronze plan (free plan). To explore each plan and its pricing details, click [here](https://rocketapi.net/pricing/).

3. **Log In**: After verifying your email, log in to your Rocket account using the credentials you provided during registration.
