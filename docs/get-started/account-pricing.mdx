## Pricing

There are 3 separate plans for Rocket, Bonze, Silver, and Gold. Along with these 3 plans, Rocket also provides custom plan to create custom plans based on requirements. The Bronze plans in Rocket is always free and will remain free. Check the [pricing page](https://rocketapi.net/pricing/) for more information.

## Account Creation

To create a new account in Rocket, follow the steps provided below.

1. **Visit Registration Page**: To get started, go to [https://app.rocketapi.net/auth/register](https://app.rocketapi.net/auth/register) for the account registration page.

2. **Provide Account Details**: In the registration page, you need to provide your name, email, and password to create a new account.

3. **Email Verification**: Once you submit the form, you'll receive an OTP (One-Time Password) in the provided email to complete the account creation process.

4. **Log In**: After verifying your email, log in to your Rocket account using the credentials you provided during registration.
