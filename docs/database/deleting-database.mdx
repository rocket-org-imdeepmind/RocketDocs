# Deleting a Database

Removing a database from Rocket is a straightforward process. Simply follow the steps outlined below:

1. **Access the Databases Page**: Log in to your Rocket account and navigate to the database section by selecting the appropriate link from the left sidebar menu.
2. **Locate the Database**: Identify the database you intend to delete from the list displayed on the page.
3. **Initiate Deletion**: Click on the delete button located at the top right corner of the selected database's tile.
4. **Confirm Deletion**: Upon clicking the delete button, a confirmation window will appear. To proceed with the deletion, click on the "Delete" button within this window.

By following these steps, you can effectively delete databases from your Rocket account.
